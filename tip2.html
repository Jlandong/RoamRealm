Tip: Drink Water Regularly

Dehydration can lead to fatigue, headaches, and other health problems.

Having a water bottle on hand ensures that you stay hydrated and refreshed during your trip.

Especially in hot climates or long-haul flights, carrying water is essential for your well-being.

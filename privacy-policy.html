Privacy Policy

At TravelNow, we value your privacy. This privacy policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you make a booking or contact us through our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to process bookings, provide customer support, send booking confirmations, and enhance your experience on our site. We may also use it for marketing purposes if you opt in to receive promotional material.

3. Data Security

Your personal data is stored securely using encryption and other safety measures. We regularly monitor our systems to ensure your data remains protected from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us with processing bookings and payments.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies may collect data such as your preferences and browsing behavior. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be reflected at the bottom of the policy.
